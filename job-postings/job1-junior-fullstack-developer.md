# Sample Job Posting - Junior Full Stack Developer

## Company: Tech Innovations Philippines Inc.
**Location:** Manila, Philippines (Hybrid)  
**Job Type:** Full-time  
**Salary Range:** PHP 25,000 - 35,000/month

---

## About the Company
Tech Innovations Philippines is a growing software development company specializing in web applications and enterprise solutions. We work with clients across various industries including education, healthcare, and e-commerce.

---

## Job Description
We are seeking a talented Junior Full Stack Developer to join our development team. The ideal candidate will have a strong foundation in web development technologies and a passion for creating user-friendly applications.

---

## Key Responsibilities
- Develop and maintain web applications using modern frameworks
- Collaborate with senior developers on full-stack projects
- Write clean, maintainable, and efficient code
- Participate in code reviews and team meetings
- Debug and resolve technical issues
- Contribute to system design and architecture discussions

---

## Essential Requirements

### Technical Skills
- **Frontend:** HTML, CSS, JavaScript
- **Backend:** PHP or Node.js experience
- **Database:** MySQL or PostgreSQL
- **Version Control:** Git/GitHub
- **Frameworks:** Experience with Bootstrap or similar CSS frameworks

### Education
- Bachelor's degree in Information Technology, Computer Science, or related field
- Recent graduate or final year student considered

### Experience
- 1-2 years of web development experience OR
- Strong academic projects demonstrating full-stack capabilities
- Portfolio of completed projects (GitHub repositories)

---

## Desirable Requirements
- Experience with React or Next.js
- Understanding of RESTful APIs
- Knowledge of Agile/Scrum methodologies
- Experience with cloud platforms (Vercel, Firebase, AWS)
- UI/UX design sensibility
- AI/ML integration experience (bonus)

---

## Key Selection Criteria

### Technical Competency (40%)
- Proficiency in required programming languages
- Understanding of full-stack development concepts
- Code quality and best practices
- Problem-solving abilities

### Project Experience (30%)
- Quality of portfolio projects
- Complexity of problems solved
- Evidence of independent learning
- Contribution to team projects

### Cultural Fit (20%)
- Communication skills
- Team collaboration
- Learning mindset
- Adaptability and growth potential

### Professional Readiness (10%)
- Work authorization for Philippines
- Availability to start
- Salary expectations alignment
- Location compatibility (hybrid work)

---

## What We Offer
- Competitive salary with performance bonuses
- Health insurance coverage
- Flexible hybrid work arrangement
- Professional development opportunities
- Modern tech stack and tools
- Collaborative and supportive team environment
- Career growth path to senior positions

---

## Interview Process
1. **Initial Screening (15 min):** HR call to discuss background and expectations
2. **Technical Assessment (45 min):** Coding challenge and technical discussion
3. **Team Interview (30 min):** Meet with development team and project manager
4. **Final Interview (20 min):** Discussion with CTO about role fit and career goals

---

## How to Apply
Send your resume, portfolio (GitHub link), and a brief cover letter explaining why you're interested in this role to: careers@techinnovationsph.com

**Application Deadline:** Open until filled

---

## Notes for Interview Preparation
This role is perfect for someone with:
- Strong academic foundation in web development
- Real-world project experience (like the University Clearance System)
- Passion for learning new technologies
- Collaborative team spirit
- Growth mindset and career ambition

**Focus Areas for Interview:**
- Discuss your University Clearance Management System project in detail
- Highlight your Digital Twin MCP Server experience (shows AI/ML bonus)
- Emphasize your full-stack capabilities (PHP, JavaScript, MySQL)
- Demonstrate your learning through modern tech (Next.js, TypeScript)
- Show your problem-solving process with specific examples
